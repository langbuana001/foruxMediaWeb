Who We Are

We are Forux Media, created at first as a response to Green River College's bad (at least that's how we consider it) newspaper, The Current. We are looking to replace The Current as the main news-hub for every student in campus. The way to do that however, brought us to a bigger goal: Revolutionize The News.



Forux was created not just to be a tech company. At it's core, it's a media company. Forux is trying to improve our reader's experience when they're reading the news. In a grander scale, we are trying to make the news as friendly looking as possible.



Our Guiding Philosophy

We are here to revolutionize the way people look at the news. And as every revolution in human history, we need a driving philosophy to guide us through the long road ahead.



Unlike any other media, which bombarded their readers with elements after elements, information after information, bright contrast, and millions of colors mashed up together. We believe, by keeping our design focused and in-context with our cause, we can provide an alternative to the news reader. Or even convert media hater, into lover.



If you have browsed around our site, as you can see, we try to keep things simple and clean. Overcomplexity and bombardment of elements is something we heavily avoided. We are no art student, but if this would help: We prefer a minimalist style approach.
